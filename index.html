want to know.

Our proposal packages start at $375 for a 1-hour session including 50+ professionally edited photos, a planning consultation, and a private online gallery delivered within 48–72 hours. Our most popular package is $650 for 2 hours with a 24–48hr sneak peek.

We use telephoto zoom lenses that let us shoot from 50–100 feet away without being noticed. We arrive early to scout angles and hiding spots, dress casually like tourists, and never make eye contact or draw attention. In 50+ proposals, we've never been spotted.

Sneak peeks arrive within 24–48 hours for most packages (same-day for Grand Proposal). Full edited galleries are delivered within 48–72 hours via your private Pixieset gallery with full-resolution download access. Weddings are delivered within 2–3 weeks.

Absolutely — we're available nationwide and internationally. We've shot proposals across Florida and beyond. Travel fees apply outside Central Florida and are included in a custom quote. See our Hidden Locations Guide for worldwide destination pricing.

Contact us or book directly at canonmomentphotographyllc.pixieset.com/booking. A 50% non-refundable retainer holds your date. Balance is due on or before your session day. Setup packages require 4 weeks advance booking.

We watch the forecast obsessively. We'll contact you 24–48 hours before if rain looks severe. Light overcast is actually beautiful for portraits. For heavy rain, we'll reschedule at no extra charge with 72 hours notice.
